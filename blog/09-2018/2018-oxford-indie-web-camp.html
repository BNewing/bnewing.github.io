2018 Oxford Indie Web Camp

Published on 23/09/2018

Day One

Level 1

Indie web sign-in is where you create a two-way link between your own personal site and other sites like GitHub/Twitter etc. To do this, make sure that your site has a link to your profiles on this sites with rel="me" in the mark-up, and that there is a link back to your personal site on those other sites. Once you've pushed it live, you can check the links at: IndieWebify.me

Level 2

Microformats are semantic classes that you can add onto your HTML that allow for data to be picked up by other things. You add them to blog posts.

Things Implemented

After going through the documentation up to level 2, I got a bit stuck! So I drew some diagrams and Garrett had a look at them with me and helped explain the bits that didn't make sense. I then set up webmentions.io and brid.gy for collecting webmentions, and Dan and I used the command line to regenerate his webmention from his recent repost of my blog. I then got work getting a fetch up and running to collect data from these and display them on my blog post. By the end of day one, I managed to get a face wall up at the bottom of the blog post, which was pretty cool!
