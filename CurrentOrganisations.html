What do I do at the moment?

At present, I'm currently involved with the following organisations:

Code First: Girls

I am the Programmes Intern at Code First: Girls. I recruit volunteers, am a point of contact for our community, and organise and co-ordinate Code First: Girls courses across the UK and Ireland

Three Rings CIC

I am a Support Volunteer at Three Rings CIC. Once a week, I man the support e-mail inbox, and write the weekly round-up to keep the rest of the Three Rings team up to date on what the support team does!

PoleFit London

I am also one of Sam King's pole students at Polefit Stockwell. My current nemesis moves are handsprings, ayesha and cross-ankle release!
