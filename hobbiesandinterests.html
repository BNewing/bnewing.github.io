Interests and Hobbies

Here is a pretty random, odd collection of things that I like, and do when I get the time:



Pole Dancing

I've been pole dancing for over 4 years now! Current nemesis moves are handsprings, ayesha and cross ankle release. Currently going to pole schools in London and Oxford.

Feeding Geese

Geese are my favourite animals. They're grumpy and moody and fight a lot and they're a lot of fun to watch. Barnacle geese are the best geese, but there used to be lots of Canada Geese at Warwick uni.

Coding!

I first started coding properly when I did a Code First: Girls course in Spring 2016. This website is me playing around with code and is mostly a place for me to play with and practise new things!
