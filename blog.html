Internships and Switching into Tech Blog Posts

Throughout the process of becoming a developer, I've been writing various blogs about how I've found it and what my internships have been like.

  • Success stories: women in tech awards - posted on the Oxford Computer Consultants Blog. Discussing the awards I was nominated for and won in 2017, and the value in these types of awards.
  • One Year on From My HTML/CSS Course - posted on the Code First: Girls blog. Reflecting on the past year since taking part in a Code First: Girls HTML/CSS coding course at Level39 in Canary Wharf. Also includes lots of tips for those interested in becoming developers coming from a non-computer-science degree background!
  • Introducing Our Front End Intern - posted on the Oxford Computer Consultants Blog. Introducing myself as a Front End Intern at OCC, what I'm doing there so far and what it's been like!
  • Web Dev Bev Out - posted on the Daily Zooniverse. A round-up of what I did during my internship with the Zooniverse!
  • A Month in at the Zoo - posted on the Daily Zooniverse. A blog talking about the challenges I've found with programming, one month into my Zooniverse Web Developer Internship.
  • Meet Beverley - posted on the Daily Zooniverse. A blog about what my first two weeks of my Zooniverse internship have been like.
  • Why I didn't Pursue a STEM subject and How I got Back into Coding - posted on Gender and the City. In this post, I discuss why I gave up on coding as a teenager, and how I got back into it after an English and German degree.
